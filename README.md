Pocket Critters by Taxalottle Syndicate!
Pocket critters is a pokemon-like game featuring pokemon from pokengine.org
This game was originally made for Hack Club Ceiling in March 2026 after 24 hours of work!
It was then remade and submitted to Hack Club Milkyway in May 2026 after 73 more hours of work!
This brings the total of the game up to roughly 97 hours! (My biggest project yet)
This game has so much more room for expansion but this is it for now.
I really enjoyed working on this and made something new that I was heavily invested in!

To play the game
Use WASD to move around. You can click on different buttons and stuff.
When you encounter critters in the tall grass you can catch them, but its not a 100% chance!
You can't catch critters in trainer battles or run from them! You have to prove your might.
You can rest on benches to heal up your team all the way.
Pause the game with escape to save and look at you team.
All sorts of other fun things to discover!
